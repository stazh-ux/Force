    Help & About Force Quit

    What is Force Quit? This app is designed to help you quit porn addiction by gamifying your journey. Track your progress, stay motivated, and build healthier habits one day at a time.

    Key Features:

    • Authentication: Sign up, log in, or continue as a guest to save your progress.
    • Profile: Set your name and profile picture to personalize your experience.
    • Survival Stats: Monitor your stopwatch, days survived, energy level, energy per second, and login streaks. Claim rewards for consistent logins.
    • Quit Date: Set or update your quit date and time honestly to track your journey.
    • Daily Missions: Complete 3 tasks each day. Finishing them increases your energy rate, helping you progress faster.
    • Motivation Boost: Get random motivational messages to stay inspired.
    • Rewards: Unlock achievements based on your milestones.
    • Survival Calendar: Visualize your clean days on a calendar.
    • Relapse & Reset: If you relapse, reset your progress and try again—honesty is key.

    Tip: Completing daily tasks boosts your energy rate, making it easier to level up. Stay consistent and build your future!
